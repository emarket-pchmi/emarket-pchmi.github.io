About us

Our business is all about food.

We conveniently bring all your favourite products right to your doorstep! And also in a timely manner. Just log in, select the desired foods and beverages, add your contact data and choose your method of payment and how fast you would like to have your delivery. Leave the rest to us. The company was established in 2022, inspired by the ever-expanding online-shopping industry. Especially now, following the events of 2020, people demand and expect traditional businesses to evolve and digitalise. E-Market does not own any physical stores whatsoever. We store all our products in several designated warehouse buildings in and around Sofia, Bulgaria. All logistical operations are organized in such a way which guarantees that the products reach us in less than 2 days after production.
